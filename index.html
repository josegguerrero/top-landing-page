An Overview of One Piece

It was a time when pirates ruled the seas. Several bands of pirates were battling over the great hidden treasure, One Piece, which was left by the now legendary pirate captain, Gold Roger. There was a young boy who admired the pirates, his name was Monkey D. Luffy. One day, he mistakenly eats the devil's fruit and turns himself into a rubber human being. Ten years have passed since that incident. Luffy sets out to sail all alone. He sets out to become a great pirate captain and that's when his great adventure begins. "I'm going to be the Pirate King! I'm going to get One Piece!". (Source: anidb.net)

Random Facts

It was originally supposed to last only 5 years.

Oda started as an artist to avoid getting a "real job".

There are tons of dedicated One Piece theme parks, exhibits, and restaurants.

Luffy is an ambassador for the 2020 Tokyo Summer Olympics.
